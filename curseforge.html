classic 3D

Classic 3D is a resource pack that turns the default models into more complex and detailed blocks, while still looking like Minecraft. The latest version of this resource pack works in 1.13-1.17, but make sure you get the right one. for example, if you are playing on 1.16.5, get the 1.16 version.

classic 3D - mod support

Classic 3D mod support adds the level of detail present in Classic 3D, to mods like Quark, Biomes-O-Plenty, and many more.
